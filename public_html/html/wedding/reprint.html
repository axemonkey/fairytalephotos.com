A typical wedding reprint order might consist of upwards of 100 prints of many different sizes. These prints will be destined for anything from ten upward different people.

All our prints are checked to ensure they are in fact the correct print (photo-processors have been known to make errors, particularly on large and complex orders) and any error rectified.
Then each is marked with the film number and print number – this is done so that reprints can be ordered in future.
Then stamped with our personalised stamp (so that in five years time the person trying to obtain another copy knows who to contact).
And, finally each individual's order is packaged individually – to simplify distribution.

So you can see this all takes considerable time to carry out before the order is ready for collection.
