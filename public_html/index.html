Introduction

Fairytale Photos is a long established family photography business providing quality photographic services at affordable prices.




Founded by Angela Light in 1988, Fairtyale Photos has always aimed to offer a friendly personal service to it's customers, together with excellent value. Angela's daughter, Saffron, acts as her assistant photographer at weddings.

Fairytale Photos offers a wide range of photographic services from our shop and studio in St Leonards-on-sea, East Sussex (above), covering Wedding and Portrait photography – and much more. We invite you to explore the site and find out for yourself.
